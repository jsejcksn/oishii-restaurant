Oishii, located in Houston, Texas (near Greenway Plaza), is a family-owned restaurant offering the finest in Japanese food, including sushi, sashimi, favorite Japanese cooked entrees—such as tempura, teriyaki and soba noodle dishes—special salads, and outstanding desserts. We have an excellent selection of fine wines, sake, and beer. We also specialize in take-out and off-premises catering. Come in and experience our quick and delicious Japanese food. You will find out why our loyal customers return again and again. We offer dine-in service as well as to go order so you can enjoy your meal either in our well-staffed restaurant, or in the comfort of your home.

With Wi-Fi now available, you can enjoy the greatest Japanese food in town while keeping in touch with the business world.
